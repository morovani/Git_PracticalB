3/4c white sugar
1/3c unsweetened cocoa powder
1pinch salt
1/3c boiling water
3 1/2c milk
3/4teaspoon vanilla extract
1/2c half-and-half cream

1. Combine sugar, cocoa powder, salt, and boiling water to a saucepan, whisking until smooth.
2. Bring to a simmer for ~2minutes, stirring continuously.
3. Stir in milk and heat until hot, but not boiling.
4. Remove from heat, add vanilla.
5. Pour into mug, add half-and-half to taste, as well as marshmallows.

Garnish
1. Choco powder
2. Whipped cream
3. Crushed Cookies
